[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category] Under what circumstances can researchers and technicians of Academia Sinica take up positions in profit-making enterprises?

Academia Sinica's researchers and technicians must first obtain permission from Academia Sinica if they want to take up profit-making business positions. Once this condition is met, there are five possible scenarios. The first is to be appointed or recommended by the public equity management agency to be a director, supervisor or similar position of the government's direct or indirect investment enterprise. The second is that the person who leads the R&D technology can be a director of a start-up company that has a technology transfer contract with the Academia Sinica, but within the validity period of the contract. The third is that they use Academia Sinica's R&D achievements to start a business and become entrepreneurs, but if they have an administrative position in Academia Sinica, they cannot do so. The fourth is that they can serve as independent directors of state-owned enterprises, listed companies, banks or securities subsidiaries that have an industry-research cooperation contract with Academia Sinica, but similarly, they cannot do so if they have administrative positions in Academia Sinica. The last situation is that they can take up non-operating business positions in profit-making enterprises that have a contract with Academia Sinica within the validity period of the contract. If they have not signed a technology transfer or research funding contract with Academia Sinica, their concurrent tenure in the same company cannot exceed one year, but it can be extended for one year.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category] How long is the period for the R&D technology provider of Academia Sinica to serve as the director of the start-up company?

The R&D technology provider of Academia Sinica is the director of the start-up company, and the concurrent term must be within the validity period of the technology transfer contract.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] If a researcher of Academia Sinica concurrently serves as a representative of a public stock or a director of a government investment enterprise, what laws and regulations on avoidance of interests need to be complied with?

If they or their family members have obtained property interests exceeding NT$150,000 from these profit-making enterprises in the past year, or hold equity interests in these profit-making enterprises (unless the equity does not exceed 5% of the listed company), they must disclose . Then, if they or someone in their family are the principals, directors, supervisors or managers of those for-profit businesses, they also need to disclose. For the latter, if these positions are used to obtain or hold the property interests or equity mentioned in the previous two, it is also a matter that needs to be disclosed. If they disclose the interest relationship because of these part-time positions, if it is determined that there is a conflict of interest that cannot be avoided, they can no longer part-time jobs. And within two years after they finish these part-time positions, they need to avoid procurement or planning review business related to their original part-time start-up or for-profit enterprises and their related enterprises. However, when such avoidance may affect fair competition or public interest, it can be reported to Academia Sinica for approval before avoiding it. Our salary structure here, whether it is a researcher or a research technician, is composed of two parts: monthly salary and bonus. The monthly salary includes the basic salary, which is the annual merit salary, as well as professional bonuses. But it should be noted that some items will be deducted from the monthly salary, such as public insurance, pension fund, health insurance, and if you live in public housing, rent allowance will also be deducted. In addition, there will be additional academic research bonuses and year-end work bonuses every year. In particular, if you are a specially-appointed researcher or an approved research technician, you will also be given a research grant; if you are also a supervisor, you can also give additional compensation to the supervisor according to your title. So basically, the salary structure is quite rich.

When researchers or research technicians arrive or leave their posts, the Human Resources Office of Academia Sinica handles the insurance addition or withdrawal of insurance for public and educational personnel.

[Insurance] What are the items that can be applied for in public and educational personnel insurance?

The payment items that public and educational personnel insurance can apply for include disability benefits, old-age benefits, death benefits, funeral benefits for family members, maternity benefits (only for women), and childcare benefits without pay.

[Insurance] Is there any difference between national health insurance and civil servants insurance?

Some of the key differences between national health insurance and civil servants insurance are their coverage and types of benefits. Our public and educational personnel insurance mainly includes disability benefits, old-age benefits, death benefits, family funeral benefits, maternity benefits (only for women), childcare leave without pay benefits, etc., which only provide insurance for individuals. On the other hand, national health insurance provides medical insurance for the individual and his family members, covering comprehensive medical services such as daily medical treatment and emergency treatment. Therefore, basically, public and educational personnel insurance focuses on personal benefits, while national health insurance focuses more on medical protection.

[Insurance] What medical services are covered by national health insurance?

The medical services covered by the national health insurance include comprehensive medical services such as daily medical visits and emergency services.

[Insurance] What does contract employee insurance include?

There are two kinds of insurance for contract employees: labor insurance and national health insurance. Labor insurance can be divided into general accident insurance, employment insurance and occupational accident insurance. General accident insurance has five types of benefits: maternity, injury, disability, old age and death. Employment insurance includes two kinds of benefits, childcare leave without pay allowance and unemployment. Occupational accident insurance has four benefits: injury, medical treatment, disability and death. As for the national health insurance, it provides daily medical and emergency services.

At the same time, we will also handle the addition or withdrawal of insurance for you and your dependents through our personnel office in accordance with the requirements of the National Health Insurance Law. This ensures that you can enjoy safety protection and receive medical services in a timely manner during or after you leave your job.
