中央研究院 人事 FAQ

[Part-time job control status (1) Prohibited category] Can the researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica hold other public positions concurrently?

Unless otherwise specified by law, researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica cannot concurrently hold other public positions.

[Part-time job control status (1) Prohibited category] If it is a licensed occupation, can the researchers and research technicians of the Academia Sinica work concurrently?

The researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica are generally not allowed to concurrently serve as licensed occupations unless they are specifically stipulated by laws and regulations. Licensed occupations refer to jobs that require licenses or certificates, such as doctors or lawyers, are licensed occupations. However, if they participate in social welfare activities or other irregular and non-continuous work outside of working hours, or their sideline job is the content of teaching or research work identified by Academia Sinica, these situations are acceptable.

[Part-time job control status (1) Prohibited category] For social welfare activities or non-recurring work, can the researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica do it outside of working hours?

Yes, as long as they perform social service activities or other infrequent, non-sustainable work outside of working hours and these activities do not interfere with their primary work.

[Part-time job control status (1) Prohibited category] Can the researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica concurrently work for teaching or research work?

If the Academia Sinica confirms that the job belongs to the category of teaching or research after review, then researchers and research technicians can be employed concurrently.

[Part-time job control status (1) Prohibited category] Can the researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica concurrently hold positions in for-profit enterprises? If it is approved by Academia Sinica, is it possible?

In principle, researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica cannot concurrently hold positions in profit-making enterprises. But if it is first approved by the Academia Sinica, then it can be done.

[Part-time job control status (1) Prohibited category] If they concurrently serve as editors of academic journals, series of books, and textbook publishing organizations set by the Ministry of Education, can the researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica concurrently serve?

Yes, as long as the work does not involve a for-profit business. However, if it is a specific profit-making position, it needs to be approved by the Academia Sinica before it can be used concurrently.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category] What kind of part-time jobs can researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica do?

After the researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica are approved, they can assume the positions of several profit-making enterprises. First of all, they can be selected by public stock equity management institutions as representative public stocks, or be recommended to serve as directors, supervisors, and other similar positions in direct or indirect government investment enterprises. In addition, if they are the main R&D technology providers, they can serve as directors of start-up companies that have a technology transfer contract with Academia Sinica, but the concurrent term must be within the validity period of the contract. Similarly, if they are the main R&D technology providers and use the results of Academia Sinica to participate in the establishment of new businesses, they can act as initiators, but if they also hold administrative positions in Academia Sinica, they cannot do so. Furthermore, as independent directors, they can work in state-owned enterprises, listed companies, banks and other subsidiaries 100% owned by financial holding companies that have an industry-research cooperation contract with Academia Sinica, but they cannot also hold administrative positions in Academia Sinica. Finally, if it is based on the needs of scientific research, they can take up non-executive business positions in a profit-making enterprise that has a contract with Academia Sinica, and the concurrent employment period must also be within the validity period of the contract. If they have not signed a contract with Academia Sinica for technology transfer or funding research, then their concurrent tenure in the same for-profit enterprise can only be one year, and can be extended by one year at most.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category] If it is a major R&D technology provider, under what circumstances can it serve as a director of a start-up company?

If you are the main R&D technology provider, there are two situations in which you can also serve as the director of the start-up company. First, if you have signed a technology transfer contract with Academia Sinica, you can serve as a director of the company, but the concurrent term must be within the valid period of the contract. Second, if you can use the research and development achievements of Academia Sinica to participate in the creation of new businesses, you can also serve as the sponsor of the new company. However, if you concurrently hold the administrative position of Academia Sinica, then you cannot carry out these activities.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category] Can researchers from Academia Sinica participate in the establishment of new businesses and act as initiators?

If the researchers of the Academia Sinica are the main R&D technology providers and use the R&D results of the Academia Sinica to create new businesses, they can be the initiators of this new business. However, if the researcher also holds the administrative position of Academia Sinica, he cannot be the sponsor.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category] In which companies can I serve as an independent director, and if I am an administrative staff of Academia Sinica, can I serve as an independent director?

The researchers and research technicians of Academia Sinica can serve as independent directors in state-owned enterprises, listed companies, wholly-owned subsidiaries of financial holding companies such as banks, securities, insurance, and comprehensive securities companies that have entered into an industry-research cooperation contract with Academia Sinica, and can also serve as independent directors in unlisted public companies that plan to apply for listing through the resolution of the board of directors or shareholders' meeting. However, researchers and research technicians who concurrently hold administrative positions in Academia Sinica cannot hold such positions.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category] For the needs of scientific research, what kind of profit-making careers can the researchers of Academia Sinica concurrently hold? How long is this concurrent tenure?

Researchers of Academia Sinica may concurrently serve as non-executive business positions of profit-making enterprises contracted with Academia Sinica for scientific research. As for the concurrent term, it must be within the validity period of the contract. However, if it is a for-profit enterprise that has not signed a technology transfer or research funding contract with Academia Sinica, the concurrent tenure period for the same for-profit enterprise can only be one year, and can only be extended by one year at most.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - dismissal registration] If I held a position in a profit-making enterprise before working in Academia Sinica, how should I register for dismissal?

Before you start working at Academia Sinica, you need to submit a written resignation and complete the dismissal registration within three months, and then submit the necessary supporting documents to Academia Sinica. If there are special circumstances that prevent you from completing the registration within the time limit, the Academia Sinica may allow you to extend it, but it can only be extended for three months at most. Remember that you cannot participate in business activities or receive remuneration until you complete the dismissal registration.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - dismissal registration] If I cannot complete the dismissal registration within 3 months, can I apply for an extension?

Yes, if you have special circumstances that prevent you from completing the dismissal registration within three months, and you have obtained the consent of Academia Sinica, you can apply for an extension. However, it should be noted that the extension period can only be up to three months. And before completing the dismissal registration, you cannot participate in the business or receive remuneration.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - dismissal registration] Can I participate in the operation of the for-profit business or receive remuneration before completing the discharge registration?

No, you are not allowed to participate in the operation of profit-making enterprises and receive remuneration before completing the dismissal registration.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - dismissal registration] What relevant supporting documents do I need to submit to Academia Sinica to complete the dismissal registration?

You need to submit proof of resignation to Academia Sinica to complete the dismissal registration.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - dismissal registration] In case of special circumstances, how long can the dismissal registration be extended?

If there are special circumstances, the dismissal registration can be extended to a maximum of three months.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - handling procedures] If a researcher of Academia Sinica wants to serve as a public stock representative or a director of a government investment enterprise, what procedures do they need to go through?

If the researchers of Academia Sinica want to concurrently act as public stock representatives or directors of government investment enterprises, they need to obtain a letter from the public stock equity management agency, then obtain the consent of the director of the research institute or research center to which they belong, and finally need the approval of Academia Sinica.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - handling procedures] What conditions or steps do researchers need to meet to serve as directors or non-executive business positions of start-up companies?

If a researcher wants to serve as a director of a start-up company or a non-executive business position with special permission from Academia Sinica, first of all, a part-time start-up company or a profit-making business needs a letter. Next, part-timers must fill in the "Academia Sinica Personnel Need Part-time Permission for Technology Transfer and Scientific Research" and attach relevant forms. The completed permission letter and related forms will be submitted to the business meeting of the affiliated research institute or research center for review. If the review is approved, the document will be forwarded to the Academia Sinica Conflict of Interest Management Committee for review. The whole process needs to be finally approved by the Academia Sinica.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - handling procedures] If researchers at Academia Sinica want to start a new business, how should they apply?

If a researcher at Academia Sinica wants to start a new business, he needs to first fill out a permission form, and then apply to his research institute or research center, and then just follow the procedures mentioned above.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - handling procedures] What should I pay attention to if I want to concurrently serve as an independent director of a profit-making business?

To concurrently serve as an independent director of a profit-making enterprise, first, when nominated for appointment, the consent of the director of the research institute or research center to which he belongs must be obtained, and then the approval of the Academia Sinica must be obtained. After being elected, the for-profit enterprise must be invited to hold the first board meeting after the shareholders' meeting, and complete the industry-research cooperation contract with Academia Sinica within three months from the date of election, and agree to pay academic rewards. Matters concerning contracts and rewards shall be counted from the date of appointment. If the process is completed within the specified time within three months, the election will be considered legal. If it is not completed on time, Academia Sinica has the right to refuse to approve concurrently serving as an independent director. In addition, if concurrently serving as an independent director results in other related work, an application must be submitted, and the director of the research institute or research center to which he belongs must agree, and then be approved by the Academia Sinica.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - handling procedures] Are there any regulations regarding the selection of independent directors and the payment of academic rewards?

Yes, if a researcher or technician of Academia Sinica wants to serve as an independent director of a profit-making enterprise, he must first obtain the consent of the director of the research institute or research center to which he belongs, and must obtain the approval of Academia Sinica. When this person is elected as an independent director, he needs to sign an industry-research cooperation contract with Academia Sinica and agree to pay academic rewards with the for-profit enterprise’s first board meeting after the shareholders’ meeting, that is, within three months from the date of election. This resolution shall be reflected in the minutes of the Board of Directors. If the board of directors cannot complete the above-mentioned things within three months, the Academia Sinica will reject the person's application to concurrently serve as an independent director. If the company serving as the director fails to complete the signing of the industry-research cooperation contract and the payment of the academic reward within the three-month period, the part-time agreement will become invalid on the second day after the expiration. In addition, if this person has other relevant positions arising from concurrently serving as an independent director, he must submit an application again, and must obtain the consent of the director of the research institute or research center to which he belongs, as well as the approval of the Academia Sinica.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - handling procedures] What will happen if the part-time for-profit enterprise fails to complete the payment of the scholarship within the stipulated period?

If the part-time profit-making enterprise fails to complete the signing of the industry-research cooperation contract and pay the academic feedback within the prescribed three months, then the Academia Sinica’s consent to the part-time job will be invalid from the day after the expiration of the three-month period. In other words, the eligibility for part-time employment will be revoked.

[Part-time job control style (2) special permission category - handling procedures] What kind of application and review process is required for the relevant positions derived from concurrently serving as an independent director?

Relevant positions derived from concurrently serving as independent directors need to apply first, then get the consent of the director of the research institute or research center to which they belong, and finally send it to the Academia Sinica for review. Only with the approval of Academia Sinica can the application be considered approved.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] If the researchers and technicians of Academia Sinica also serve as public stock representatives or government investment undertakings, how can they avoid interests?

If the researchers and technicians of Academia Sinica concurrently serve as representatives of public shares or government investment undertakings, they should avoid interests in accordance with the provisions of the Law on Avoidance of Conflicts of Interest for Public Officials. If these employees are also directors of start-up companies or profit-making enterprises, they also need to disclose whether they have the following interest relations with these companies or enterprises. For example, if they themselves, their spouses or minor children have received property interests of more than NT$150,000 from these profit-making enterprises in the past year, or held equity in these enterprises, they must be disclosed. But if they hold less than 5% of the shares of listed companies, this rule does not apply. Other circumstances that need to be disclosed include that they or their family members hold important positions in these for-profit enterprises; or they or their spouses or minor children are the trustees or agents of property for benefits. Finally, if they also start new businesses, they also need to disclose potential interests in these new businesses. After disclosure, if it is determined that there is a conflict of interest and no interest avoidance measures can be taken, then they cannot work part-time. Within two years of terminating part-time employment, they also need to avoid all procurement or planning reviews related to start-up companies, for-profit enterprises and related enterprises where they previously worked part-time. However, if the avoidance would hinder fair competition or the public interest, they can report to the hospital for exemption. If each unit finds that there are people who have not avoided interests, they should also notify these researchers and research technicians to avoid interests.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] If the researchers and technicians of Academia Sinica also serve as directors or other non-executive business positions of start-up companies, what kind of interest relationship do they need to disclose?

If the research and technical personnel of Academia Sinica also serve as directors or other non-executive business positions of start-up companies, they need to disclose whether they have the following interest relationships with start-up companies and profit-making enterprises. First, if you, your spouse, or your minor children have obtained property interests exceeding NT$150,000 from the profit-making enterprise in the previous year, or hold equity in the company, you must disclose it, but if the equity in the listed (cabinet) company is less than 5%, it is not necessary. Second, if you or your spouse, children, parents, grandparents, grandchildren, or siblings hold the position of person in charge, director, supervisor, or manager of the company, you also need to disclose. Third, if the properties entrusted or represented by the self, spouse, or minor children involve the above two types of interests or equity, it is also considered that the property interests or equity of the self, spouse, or minor children need to be disclosed.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] If my family and I have obtained property interests exceeding NT$150,000 from this profit-making enterprise in the past year, or held equity in this profit-making enterprise, do we need to disclose it?

If you and your spouse or minor children have received property interests exceeding NT$150,000 from that for-profit enterprise in the past year, or held equity interests in that for-profit enterprise, you must disclose it. However, if the company holds less than 5% of the shares of a listed or OTC company, there is no need to disclose it.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] If I concurrently serve as the promoter of a new business venture, what potential interests do I need to disclose?

If you also serve as the promoter of a new venture, you need to disclose the possible interest relationship with this new venture. As for the specific content that needs to be disclosed, it is up to the Academia Sinica to decide.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] How will I be punished if a conflict of interest is discovered after disclosure?

If after disclosure it turns out that you have a conflict of interest and cannot take appropriate steps to avoid it, the rule is that you will not be able to hold part-time work. Furthermore, within two years after you stop working part-time, you should avoid engaging in any procurement or plan review business related to your original part-time company or business behavior. However, if such avoidance is not conducive to fair competition or public interest, you may apply for the consent of Academia Sinica to exempt such avoidance. If the unit finds that you should but fail to avoid a conflict of interest, they have the right to ask you to withdraw immediately.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] What matters need to be avoided within two years after the part-time job is terminated?

Within two years after the part-time job ends, it is necessary to avoid business related to procurement or plan review with the start-up company, profit-making enterprise, and their affiliated companies that were originally part-time jobs. However, if doing so is detrimental to fair competition or the public interest, it can be reported to Academia Sinica, and there is no need to avoid it after obtaining consent.

[Part-time job control mode (2) special permission category - avoidance of interests, disclosure of interests] If there is something that needs to be avoided but is not avoided, how will the unit deal with it?

If it is found that a researcher or research technician should avoid certain things but has not avoided, then the relevant unit will require this person to avoid.

[Part-time job control pattern (2) special permission category-academic reward] If I am a researcher at Academia Sinica and also serve as a director of a start-up company, how is the academic reward calculated?

If you are a researcher at Academia Sinica and concurrently serve as a director of a start-up company, the academic reward is calculated based on 15% of the concurrent director fee you receive. Then you need to pay the academic reward in full to the account designated by Academia Sinica within two months after receiving the concurrent appointment approval notice from Academia Sinica.

[Part-time job control pattern (2) special permission category-academic reward] How long is the academic reward payment period? When do I need to pay?

The payment time of academic rewards is as follows. If you are a director of a start-up company or a non-executive business position of a profit-making enterprise, you need to pay within 2 months after receiving the part-time job approval notice from Academia Sinica. If you are an independent director of a profit-making enterprise, you need to pay within 2 months after signing the production-research cooperation contract.

[Part-time job control pattern (2) special permission category-academic reward] How is the academic reward for concurrently serving as an independent director of a for-profit enterprise calculated?

The academic reward for concurrently serving as an independent director is calculated at 20% of the part-time fee. Payment should be made within two months of the completion of the contract.

[Part-time job control pattern (2) special permission category-academic reward] Where will the paid academic rewards be used?

All academic rewards will be handed over to Academia Sinica, and then put into the scientific research fund of Academia Sinica.

[Part-time job control pattern (2) special permission category-academic reward] What happens if a startup doesn't pay academic grants on time?

If the start-up company fails to pay the academic rewards on time, Academia Sinica will cancel the company's part-time status and will use legal means to recover the unpaid academic rewards.

[Part-time job control mode (3) general license category] How do researchers and technicians of Academia Sinica apply for part-time jobs?

If the researchers and technicians of Academia Sinica want to apply for part-time jobs, they must first ensure that the part-time job will not affect their main work. Then, unless there are special regulations, they need to obtain the consent of the director of the institute or research center where they are located, and then be approved by the Academia Sinica. Later, if their part-time job expires and they want to continue the part-time job, or if the part-time job is changed, they need to submit another application for review by the Academia Sinica. Furthermore, if they want to participate in research projects funded outside Academia Sinica without using the internal work or other resources of Academia Sinica, they also need to apply for part-time jobs and get the approval of Academia Sinica in accordance with the regulations. However, if they will use the resources of Academia Sinica to participate in research outside the academy, they need to follow the key points of the work of the Academia Sinica's funded research plan.

[Part-time job control mode (3) general license category] Who needs to agree to apply for a part-time job?

The part-time job application needs to be approved by the director of the research institute or research center to which it belongs, as well as the approval of the Academia Sinica.

[Part-time job control mode (3) general license category] What should I do if I want to renew my part-time job after the term expires or if there is any change in the part-time job?

If you want to continue or change your part-time position, you must apply to Academia Sinica again and wait for approval.

[Part-time job control mode (3) general license category] If the personnel of the Academia Sinica do not use their own resources to participate in other research projects, do they need to report to the Academia Sinica for approval?

[Part-time job control mode (3) general license category] If the resources of Academia Sinica are used to participate in research projects funded outside the academy, what regulations should be followed?

If you use the resources of Academia Sinica to participate in a research project funded outside the academy, you need to follow the regulations of the Academia Sinica for accepting funding for research projects.
