中央研究院 國際學生 FAQ

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What kind of plan is the TIGP-X Pilot Program?

TIGP-X Pilot Program is a program jointly cultivated by Academia Sinica and foreign universities to cultivate international doctoral students. Its purpose is to promote international academic cooperation, attract international students, and encourage Academia Sinica researchers to cooperate with foreign academic institutions. The program also provides grants to TIGP-X international doctoral students to conduct research at Academia Sinica during the dissertation research stage, so as to increase the talent pool of doctoral students.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Who can apply for this program?

Only researchers who are assistant researchers or above in the institutes and centers of Academia Sinica can apply for this plan.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How many admissions are planned for the TIGP-X Pilot Program?

As the number of admissions must be matched with the vacancy of TIGP dormitories, it is currently impossible to specify the number of admissions.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What documents are required to apply for this program?

To apply for this program, you need to prepare the following documents: cooperation plan or doctoral dissertation proposal, doctoral student’s personal application materials, recommendation letter from the supervisor of foreign cooperation unit, and letter of consent from the doctoral student’s school. Remember to review the application form in detail before applying!

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What is the application procedure for TIGP-X Pilot Program? How long will the review process take?

The TIGP-X Pilot Program has preliminary review and review procedures. Applicants need to submit to their own research institute or center for preliminary review. Then, the applicant's research institute and center will send a letter to the International Office together with the initial review opinion (if there are more than two applications in the current period, a recommendation order must be provided). After that, the International Office will compile the application cases and send them to the Academic Consulting Association for review. The review time will take about 20 working days. Afterwards, the reexamination opinion will be reported together with the application documents to the president of Academia Sinica for approval. After the re-examination results are approved, the Academia Sinica will notify the research institutes and centers to which the researchers belong.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] When is the application deadline for the TIGP-X Pilot Program?

There are three application echelons each year, and the initial review and review procedures will be carried out. Because applicants need to submit to their own research institute or center for preliminary review, the deadline for submitting applications to the research institute or center must be confirmed by the applicant to the research institute or center. Afterwards, the deadlines for the applicant's research institute and center to send the initial review opinion letter to the International Office are the end of February, the end of June, and the end of October, respectively.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] According to the date of doctoral students' research at the hospital, how should I choose the application level?

The way to choose the application level is based on the estimated time for doctoral students to study in Academia Sinica. If it is expected to start research at Academia Sinica between April and July, the institute/center must submit an application before the end of February; if it is expected to start research at Academia Sinica between August and November, the institute/center Applications should be made before the end of June; finally, if the plan is to start research at Academia Sinica between December and March of the following year, the institute/center needs to make an application before the end of October.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] The application materials for the TIGP-X Pilot Program must be written in English, right?

Yes, the application materials for the TIGP-X Pilot Program must be written in English.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How many letters of recommendation do I need?

You need to prepare at least one recommendation letter in any form, remember to write it in English.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How many TIGP-X doctoral students can the researchers of Academia Sinica receive at most each year?

Academia Sinica researchers can receive a maximum of one TIGP-X doctoral student subsidy each year, so students should be selected carefully and limited to one case in the same echelon.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] If there is a time limit for the completion of the cooperative research project, should it be stated in the application?

Yes, if there is a time limit for the completion of the cooperative research project, you need to explain it when you apply.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] The maximum application period for research cooperation in TIGP-X Pilot Program is 3 years, right?

Yes, the maximum application period for research cooperation is 3 years. Minimum 1 year.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] If you are selected, but due to special circumstances, TIGP-X doctoral students cannot come to Taiwan as planned, do you have to re-apply and review?

Yes, if a TIGP-X doctoral student cannot come to Taiwan at the original time due to special circumstances, the co-advising professor at Academia Sinica needs to resubmit the application and accept the review.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What are the two types of TIGP-X Pilot Program awards?

The TIGP-X Pilot Program has two types of grants. The first is "TIGP-X Academia Sinica Scholarship for International Doctoral Students". Basically, the Academia Sinica and the Academia Sinica researchers (applicants) will receive NT$34,000 each month and a scholarship of 16,000 yuan. If there are special circumstances, it can be raised at the time of application. The second type is "TIGP-X Academia Sinica International Doctoral Students". The scholarships received are provided by the researchers of Academia Sinica, and the amount must meet the relevant specifications of the "Academia Sinica Scholarship Funding Points". The selected doctoral students are collectively referred to as TIGP-X doctoral students.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Can TIGP-X doctoral students only get one subsidy for round-trip air tickets?

Yes, the round-trip air ticket for TIGP-X doctoral students can only be subsidized once.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How to arrange the accommodation for TIGP-X doctoral students during their stay in Academia Sinica?

During the research period of the selected TIGP-X doctoral students at Academia Sinica, Academia Sinica will arrange to live in the TIGP dormitory for the first year, and the expenses will be borne by the TIGP-X doctoral students themselves. From the second year onwards, researchers from the Academia Sinica who serve as co-advising professors will assist in renting houses outside the academy.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What kind of subsidies can researchers of Academia Sinica be selected for the program?

Academia Sinica researchers of the selected program can apply to Academia Sinica to go abroad on business, and Academia Sinica will pay for an economy-class air ticket to and from Taiwan, and pay living expenses and office expenses (handling fees, insurance premiums) according to the "Overseas Travel Expense Reporting Points" . The air ticket money can only be used to participate in the selected TIGP-X doctoral student's thesis defense (Thesis Defense), or to visit the school of the TIGP-X doctoral student during the implementation period and discuss academic cooperation matters with their supervisors.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] After being selected as a TIGP-X doctoral student, can I continue to receive the scholarship from my research institution during my research?

Yes, after being selected as a TIGP-X doctoral student, you can still continue to receive scholarships from your own research institution during your research at Academia Sinica.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] The air ticket needs to be paid in advance, will it be refunded to our post office account later?

Yes, the air ticket for being selected as a TIGP-X doctoral student needs to be paid in advance, and then after coming to Taiwan, the payment will be refunded to your post office account after going through the verification process of Academia Sinica.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Do visa and transportation expenses need to be borne by ourselves, without subsidies?

Yes, TIGP-X doctoral students need to bear the visa and transportation expenses themselves, and Academia Sinica does not provide subsidies. However, Academia Sinica will assist you in handling visa-related matters with foreign embassies, and will also provide transportation information from the airport to the dormitory.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] When conducting thesis research at Academia Sinica, we still need to be responsible for the tuition fees of overseas schools, right?

Yes, even when you are doing thesis research at Academia Sinica, you still need to be responsible for the tuition fees of overseas schools.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How to submit the research progress report written every year to the bilateral advisors for approval?

Each year you are required to write a research progress report, which is then submitted to your bilateral advisor for review. After passing the review, it will be handed over to the International Office for review by the Academic Advisory Council. After passing the review, you can get the scholarship for the next year.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Specifically, who is responsible for the guidance and assessment of TIGP-X doctoral students' research work at Academia Sinica?

The assessment of TIGP-X doctoral student’s research work guidance in Academia Sinica is the responsibility of the applicant, that is, the student’s co-advising professor in Academia Sinica.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] If TIGP-X doctoral students need to leave the country for more than a certain period of time, what will happen to the scholarship?

If you need to leave the country for more than 2 weeks due to personal reasons, the suspension of scholarship days will be counted from the first day of departure, not two weeks after departure. So once you leave the country for more than 2 weeks, the scholarship will stop from the day you leave the country.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] When the results of my research at Academia Sinica involve intellectual property rights, what regulations do I need to abide by?

When your achievements during your research at Academia Sinica involve intellectual property rights, you need to follow the "Academia Sinica Research and Development Achievements and Utilization Methods" to deal with them.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What should I do if I am involved in violation of academic ethics during my research?

If you are involved in violations of academic ethics during the research period, it will be dealt with in accordance with the "Academia Sinica Ethics Committee Settings and Operation Points".

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Which foreign academic and research institutions can TIGP-X Pilot Program cooperate with?

TIGP-X Pilot Program can cooperate with various foreign academic and research institutions, not limited to institutions that have signed an MOU with Academia Sinica. If the student's department needs to sign the MOU in response to this plan, the research institutes and centers where the researchers of the Academia Sinica belong can also sign the MOU. This program applies to the three academic areas of Academia Sinica.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Which academic fields of Academia Sinica does this plan apply to?

This plan is applicable to the three major academic fields of Academia Sinica.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Does the TIGP-X Pilot Program have any restrictions on nationality?

The TIGP-X Pilot Program basically has no restrictions on nationality. Doctoral students, whether native or foreign, can be nominated to apply. Taiwanese students who are studying in foreign universities' doctoral programs are also eligible. However, Hong Kong and Macao students need to leave the country once every six months, and cannot join the health insurance system, and their rights and interests are less than other foreign students. Students from mainland China will not be applicable until the "Regulations on the Permission for People from the Mainland Area to Enter the Taiwan Area" are revised. As for schools in mainland China, Hong Kong, and Macao, the Academia Sinica does not encourage them due to politically sensitive factors, but if the application is helpful to the joint research between the two parties, it can still be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Can students from Hong Kong and Macao participate in this program?

Students from Hong Kong and Macao can participate in this plan, but they need to leave the country once every six months and cannot join the health insurance system. Compared with other foreign students, they have less rights and interests. In addition, when politically sensitive issues are involved, the Academia Sinica will conduct a review on a case-by-case basis.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Can students from Mainland China participate in the TIGP-X Pilot Program?

Students from Mainland China are not currently eligible for the TIGP-X Pilot Program, and they need to wait for the "Regulations on Permitting People from the Mainland Area to Enter the Taiwan Area" to be relaxed and amended before they can apply.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Do applicants have to serve as co-advising professors?

It is best for the applicant to serve as a co-supervising professor, but if some overseas universities/institutions cannot accept the co-supervising system due to strict regulations, the applicant can also strive to become a thesis committee member for doctoral students. This situation will be determined on a case-by-case basis, as long as it can be confirmed that there is a situation that promotes substantive cooperation between the two parties, it should be acceptable.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] If the applicant cannot serve as a co-advising professor, can he become a thesis committee member?

Yes, if you cannot serve as a co-advising professor, you can try to become a thesis committee member. Individual cases will be considered during the review, as long as there are facts that promote substantive cooperation between the two parties, it should be acceptable.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Can applicants pay TIGP-X Ph.

After asking the National Science Council, as long as the applicant has the fact that he actually supervises the doctoral students, he can use the award subsidy planned by the National Science Council to pay the doctoral students of TIGP-X, and the student status of the National Science Council award subsidy has no nationality limit.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What assistance will Academia Sinica provide after being selected as a TIGP-X doctoral student?

First of all, Academia Sinica will contact the foreign embassy where the selected TIGP-X doctoral students are located, and ask them to help with visa-related matters. Then, relevant information such as TIGP dormitory reservation and room payment will be provided for the first year. From the second year, the accommodation will be assisted by the co-advisor of Academia Sinica (that is, the applicant of this program) to rent a house outside the hospital. Next, the traffic reference information from Taiwan airport to TIGP dormitory will be provided. After entering the country, we will also assist in the verification of the air ticket payment. After TIGP-X doctoral students enter the country, the institute/center to which the co-advising professor of Academia Sinica belongs will provide the following assistance: 1. Assist in the application process of changing the residence visa to ARC, and remind to update ARC every year. 2. Assist in applying for accident insurance after entry. 3. After staying in Taiwan for more than 183 days, they will assist in applying for health insurance, Confucianism and related matters. 4. Apply for a post office account.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How to arrange accommodation for the selected TIGP-X doctoral students during their research?

During the research period of the selected doctoral students in the institute, Academia Sinica will arrange to live in the TIGP dormitory in the first year, and from the second year, the co-advisor of Academia Sinica (that is, the applicant of this program) will assist in renting a house outside the institute.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What reports and research results do I need to submit after being selected as a TIGP-X doctoral student?

Students selected as "TIGP-X Academia Sinica Scholarship International Doctoral Students" are required to write a research progress report every year during their stay in Academia Sinica, and after being approved by the bilateral advisors, submit it to the International Office for review to obtain the next year's scholarship. In addition, within two weeks before the completion of the TIGP-X cooperative research project and the return to the place of student status, a complete written research report needs to be submitted for reference by the International Office. Students who are selected as "TIGP-X Academia Sinica International Doctoral Students" only need to submit a complete written research report within two weeks before returning to the place of student status after the completion of the TIGP-X cooperative research project, for reference by the International Office .

Selected TIGP-X doctoral students should report to the hospital within the starting date of starting research in the hospital.

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] When was TIIP established and what is its purpose?

TIIP was established to provide young foreign scholars with internship opportunities, let them know more about Academia Sinica's international graduate program, and then improve their willingness to study.

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] Is the application period from January 1st to February 28th every year?

Yes, the annual application period is from January 1 to February 28.

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] Can I apply for this summer internship program online?

Yes, you can apply for this summer internship program through the online system. The application period is from January 1 to February 28 each year.

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] When applying, you need to choose three advisors. Is it based on your own research interests and academic experience?

Yes, when applying, you need to choose three advisors in the application system according to your research interests and academic experience.

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] The internship period is fixed for 2 months, divided into three echelons, right?

Yes, the internship period is fixed as two months, divided into three echelons: the first echelon is from May 1st to June 30th, the second echelon is from June 1st to July 31st, and the third echelon is 7 August 1st to August 31st.

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] Who can apply for this summer internship program?

Those who can apply for this summer internship program are current students of foreign nationality, including final-year undergraduate students, current students of postgraduate master’s programs, and students above the fourth grade of the five-year bachelor’s and master’s double degree system. In addition, Those with a bachelor's or master's degree may also apply. But please note that those who have already participated in the TIGP summer internship program and doctoral students are not eligible for application

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] Can I juggle other jobs during my internship?

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] Will the internship program be terminated if it violates relevant regulations or Taiwan laws?

Yes, the internship program will be terminated if you violate the relevant regulations or Taiwan laws.

[International Graduate Studies Internship Program (TIIP)] What documents do I need to submit when applying for an internship program?

When applying online for the internship program, you need to complete or submit the following documents: resume, study plan, and two letters of recommendation (automatically sent to recommenders through the application system, and they need to complete the letter of recommendation before February 28) , Upload the official transcript with the school stamp, and the English test transcript (optional).

The airfare allowance is such that the airfare will only be refunded upon successful completion of the internship. (Please compare the prices of different websites before purchasing the air ticket.) If you do not return to China for some reason after the internship, such as being officially admitted to the TIGP program, they will only subsidize your one-way economy class air ticket to Taiwan. To apply for airfare credit, you will need to submit your original proof of purchase (original), original boarding pass, flight itinerary and a copy of your passport. The refund amount of air ticket fee will be in accordance with the regulations of Academia Sinica.
