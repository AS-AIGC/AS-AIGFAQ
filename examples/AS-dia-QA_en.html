[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What kind of plan is the TIGP-X Pilot Program?

TIGP-X Pilot Program is a program jointly cultivated by Academia Sinica and foreign universities to cultivate international doctoral students. Its purpose is to promote international academic cooperation, attract international students, and encourage Academia Sinica researchers to cooperate with foreign academic institutions. The program also provides grants to TIGP-X international doctoral students to conduct research at Academia Sinica during the dissertation research stage, so as to increase the talent pool of doctoral students.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] Only which researchers from Academia Sinica are eligible to apply for this program?

Only researchers who are assistant researchers or above in the institutes and centers of Academia Sinica can apply for this plan.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] What documents are required to apply for this program?

To apply for this program, you need to prepare the following documents: cooperation plan or doctoral dissertation proposal, doctoral student’s personal application materials, recommendation letter from the supervisor of the foreign cooperation unit, and the letter of consent from the doctoral student’s school. Remember to review the application form in detail before applying!

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How long does the review process take?

The review process takes approximately 20 working days.

[TIGP-X Pilot Program] How is the application deadline arranged?

The application deadline is divided into three periods, namely the end of February, the end of June and the end of October. According to the month that doctoral students are expected to study at Academia Sinica, they can choose the appropriate application level. For example, if you plan to study at Academia Sinica from April to July, you should apply before the end of February; For research at Academia Sinica, applications should be submitted before the end of October.

According to the date of doctoral students' research at the institute, the suggestions for choosing the application level are as follows: 1. If you plan to study at Academia Sinica between August and November, you should apply before the end of June. 3. If you plan to study at Academia Sinica between December and March, you should apply before the end of October.
